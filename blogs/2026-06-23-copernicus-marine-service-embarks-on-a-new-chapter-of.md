As ocean temperatures rise, sea ice declines and coastal communities face growing environmental pressures, the demand for reliable ocean data has never been greater. Meeting that demand was a central theme of the 9th Copernicus Marine General Assembly, held in Brussels from 8–10 June as part of the first Digital Ocean Week, organised by Mercator Ocean International.
